# ForgeBench Sample Reports

These examples are synthetic, human-approved sample reports.
They are designed to show ForgeBench output shape and first-run
UX. They are not real customer reports and do not contain
proprietary code.

The cases are reproducible with the checked-in `task.md`, `patch.diff`, and
`forgebench.yml` files. They demonstrate output shape only; real anonymized
reports remain a future dogfood and beta requirement.

## Cases

- `block_case`: synthetic migration/schema change without tests.
- `low_concern_case`: synthetic docs-only change with docs policy calibration.
